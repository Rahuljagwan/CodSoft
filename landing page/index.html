Eka Hotel Nairobi

Comfort & Convenience at

Welcome to Eka, a contemporary style hotel that provides all the comfort, convenience and efficiency to the discerning traveler.

Ideally located along Mombasa road in Nairobi the capital city of Kenya, Eka Hotel Nairobi features 167 well-appointed rooms, five meeting & conference rooms, restaurant & bar, gym, swimming pool, gift shop, wellness center among other top facilities offering the best choice venue for your Business and Leisure stay.

Our Mission

To acquire the loyalty of our clients by exceeding their expectations in offering comfort, convenience and efficient services.

Vision

To be the preferred hotel recognized for providing world class affordable hospitality.

Core Values

Exceptional service, Integrity, Respect, Teamwork & Passion and Continuous Improvement

Why Choose Eka Hotel?

Location

Along Mombasa road with easy access to both Jomo Kenyatta International Airport and Wilson Airport.

Service

We pride ourselves with a dedicated team, always and happy to attend to your needs timely.

Affordability

We endeavor to offer affordable convenience, without compromising on service and quality.

Reviews

We have been rewarded numerous times by reputable organizations such as TripAdvisor.

Technology

The hotel incorporates modern technology to ensure comfort & convenience for all our guests.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Eka Hotel is affiliated with the Amrit Foundation. The Hotel and the Foundation continuously support various CSR initiative as a way of giving back to society, in line with the companies’ Sustainable Development Goals.

To participate in any of noble causes, contact us or reach out to the Amrit Foundation Trust and we will be more than happy to provide further details and information.
